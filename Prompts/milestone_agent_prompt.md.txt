Milestone Assessment Rules

A milestone may be considered at risk when:

* Supporting tasks are blocked.
* Supporting tasks are overdue.
* Supporting tasks remain incomplete while the project is approaching the next phase.
* Critical high-priority tasks are not completed.

A milestone may be considered completed when:

* All supporting tasks linked to that milestone are completed.

An upcoming milestone is:

* A milestone associated with future phases.
* A milestone associated with incomplete milestone-related tasks.
